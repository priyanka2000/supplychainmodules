Project Background

The client operates 3 manufacturing plants across India with 12 production lines producing 12 beverage SKUs across PET and Glass formats. The existing planning process was fragmented across Excel sheets and disparate tools, resulting in limited visibility, reactive decision-making, and sub-optimal capacity utilisation.

The consultant was engaged to design and build a unified, web-based supply chain planning platform that would provide real-time visibility, scenario modelling, and AI-assisted recommendations across all planning domains.
